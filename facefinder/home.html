We are a company dedicated to allowing users to discover where their face appears on the internet. We also hope to increase transparency around facial recognition.

We believe that users have a right to know where they exist, and that the most crucial aspect of privacy online is awareness. Our service is designed to give users piece of mind regarding their internet presence.

We provide a service that searches the internet for appearances of your face. We then provide you with data detailing the different places where your image is being used.
